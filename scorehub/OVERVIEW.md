# ScoreHub — Football Prediction & Community Platform

## What It Is

ScoreHub is a social platform built around football (soccer) match predictions. It lets users predict match outcomes, compete on leaderboards, follow skilled predictors, and earn money from their content — all centered on real-time match data from professional football leagues worldwide.

Think of it as a mix between a sports analytics app, a social feed (like Twitter for football predictions), and a creator monetization platform.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Football fans have always predicted match results, but there was no dedicated platform that:
- Tracks your prediction accuracy over time with detailed statistics
- Lets you build a following and reputation as a skilled predictor
- Rewards top predictors financially through ad revenue sharing
- Shows live match data so predictions are made with real context

ScoreHub brings all of this together in one place.

## Key Features

**Predictions**
Users can predict multiple outcomes for any match — not just the final result. Supported prediction types include: match result, correct score, over/under goals, both teams to score, half-time/full-time result, yellow cards, and corners. Each type carries different point values based on difficulty.

**Live Match Data**
The platform pulls real-time match data — live scores, team lineups, match statistics, and league standings — so users have full context when making or viewing predictions.

**Social Feed**
A personalised feed shows predictions from people you follow and trending predictions globally. Users can like, comment, share, and save predictions. Predictions can be shared directly to WhatsApp, Twitter, Instagram, Telegram, and more.

**Leaderboards & Badges**
Users earn points and badges based on prediction accuracy and streaks. Badges are awarded across multiple dimensions — global ranking, league-specific performance, prediction type mastery, and consecutive win streaks. The top badge (Oracle) requires earning 3 or more streak badges.

**Earnings & Monetization**
Predictors earn a share of ad revenue generated by views and clicks on their prediction content. Earnings are tracked per prediction and can be withdrawn via the platform. This creates an incentive for quality, well-reasoned predictions.

**Multi-language Support**
The platform is available in English, German, Spanish, French, and Turkish — built for a global football audience.

## The Business Model

ScoreHub monetises through ads placed around prediction content. A portion of that ad revenue is paid back to the predictors who generated the content. This creates a self-sustaining content flywheel: good predictors attract followers, followers attract ad impressions, impressions generate revenue, revenue attracts more predictors.
